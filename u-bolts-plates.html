Trailer Hardware

Stock U-Bolts and Nuts

Here at GT Trailers we have a large stock range of M10 and M12 U-bolts. Our U-bolts are made from AISI 1020 grade steel and hot dip galvanized for maximum protection. U-bolt threads are produced by thread-rolling to ensure the strength and integrity of the thread.

Our U-bolts are primarily used for trailers, but also have many other uses in the construction industry and general engineering. We also supply these sectors with custom sizes and materials to suit specific requirements. All U-bolts are supplied with nuts and lock washers.

U-Bolts

Specifications:

  • M10 and M12 sizes available
  • Made from AISI 1020 grade steel
  • Hot dip galvanized for maximum protection
  • Thread-rolled threads for strength
  • Supplied with nuts and lock washers
  • Custom sizes and materials available

U-Brackets

GT Trailers stock U-brackets are made from mild steel and have a variety of applications within the trailer manufacturing and industrial sector. We pride ourselves on our variety of stock U-brackets and welcome enquiries for different sizes, materials and finishes not in our stock range.

Plates with Holes

Do you need a plate with holes to mount your U-bolt? GT Trailers can help with that too. We have a large variety of holed plates, all ranging from single holed plates to five hole plates used in the trailer and engineering industry. Our holed plates are made from mild steel and are supplied in a BARE finish or hot-dip galvanized for maximum protection.
