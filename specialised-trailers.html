Specialised Trailers

GT Specialised Trailers

GT has developed a range of proven designs and manufacturing procedures for a number of Specialised trailers for transporting vehicles, machinery and equipment. Each commercial operator has unique requirements, so we've developed trailer designs accordingly. Some examples are pictured here.

Specialised Trailer Types

Waterblaster Trailer

Designed for commercial waterblasting operations with secure mounting points and storage solutions.

Tractor Mower Trailer

Heavy-duty trailers designed specifically for transporting tractor mowers and similar equipment.

Motorbike Trailer

Secure trailers for transporting motorcycles with tie-down points and wheel chocks.

Ride-On Trailer

Trailers designed for ride-on mowers and small equipment with ramps and secure tie-downs.

Car Trailer

Professional car transport trailers with winches, ramps, and secure wheel positioning.

Mower Trailer

Specialised trailers for commercial mowing operations with equipment storage solutions.

Applications Include

  • Diggers
  • Sweepers
  • Skid-steer excavators
  • Tractor mowers
  • Ride-on mowers
  • Car transporters
  • Motor bikes
  • Generators
  • Water-blasters

Contact us to discuss your specific specialised trailer requirements. We can design and build custom trailers tailored to your exact needs.
