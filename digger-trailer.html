GT Digger Trailer

Standard payload of 2050kg, catering for 1.8 tonne machines.
Fully hot-dip galvanised monocoque chassis with hydraulic override disc brakes.
Built tough for NZ conditions.

STANDARD FEATURES ON ALL OUR GT DIGGER & PLANT TRAILERS:

  • New Zealand fabricated and assembled
  • Monocoque chassis for strength
  • Welded steel/tread plate construction
  • Fully hot-dip galvanised
  • Heavy duty tie down points
  • Tandem axles with self-equalising leaf spring suspension
  • Hydraulic override disc braking system (2500kg) or Knott Mechanical brake system with drum brakes (3000kg and 3500kg)
  • Tread plate steps front and rear
  • Laminated marine plywood deck
  • 4 position tail gate
  • Width adjustable & slam shut ramps
  • LED lights 9-33v compatible
  • Jockey wheel or machinery stand
  • Poly bucket rest strips
  • We guarantee our range of quality trailers for 2 years
