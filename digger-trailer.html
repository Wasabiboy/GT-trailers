GT Digger Trailer: The Ultimate Machine Hauler

Stop worrying about weight limits and road stability. The GT Digger Trailer range is built tough for NZ conditions, featuring a robust, fully hot-dip galvanised monocoque chassis designed to handle the daily grind with ease. Whether you're moving a mini-digger or heavy plant, our tandem axle design and hydraulic override disc brakes ensure a smooth, compliant tow every time.

For those requiring maximum capacity, we showcase the GT Digger MAX 3500 (Digamax). Fully certified to NZ standards, the Digamax features a premium Knott braking system, providing the gold standard in safety and legal compliance for 3.5-tonne transport.

  • Certified Reliability: The Digamax 3500 is fully tested and certified to NZ standards.
  • Built to Last: Hot-dip galvanised monocoque chassis for maximum protection.
  • Serious Capacity: Optimised payloads to suit 1.7T through to heavy 2.8T machines.
  • Safety First: Premium braking systems (including Knott) for heavy-duty stopping power.

STANDARD FEATURES ON ALL OUR GT DIGGER & PLANT TRAILERS:

  • New Zealand fabricated and assembled
  • Monocoque chassis for strength
  • Welded steel/tread plate construction
  • Fully hot-dip galvanised
  • Heavy duty tie down points
  • Tandem axles with self-equalising leaf spring suspension
  • Hydraulic override disc braking system (2500kg) or mechanical brake system (3000kg and 3500kg)
  • Tread plate steps front and rear
  • Laminated marine plywood deck
  • 4 position tail gate
  • Width adjustable and slam shut ramps
  • LED lights 9-33v compatible
  • Jockey wheel or machinery stand
  • Poly bucket rest strips
  • We guarantee our range of quality trailers for 2 years
