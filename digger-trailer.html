Digger Trailer

GT Digger Trailer

This Digger Trailer offers the best versatility and value-for-money of any on the market. With a standard payload of 2050kg, trailers can cater for a 1.8 tonne machine with extra tools and parts. All components are locally sourced to ensure maximum reliability and parts availability.

Standard Features

  • Fully hot dip galvanised, monocoque chassis
  • Deck size 2950mm x 1450mm
  • 2500kg self equalising leaf spring suspension
  • Hydraulic override disc brake system
  • Heavy-duty tie-down points
  • 25mm marine ply deck
  • Width adjustable, slam shut ramps
  • Poly bucket rest strips
  • Three-position tailgates
  • 17/8 or 50mm coupling
  • Galvanised spoked wheels
  • Heavy-duty Jockey Wheel
  • LED lights are standard
  • Two high-tensile safety chains with rated shackles
  • Tread plate steps front and rear

Model Specifications

MODEL: DIG10x5TA
PAYLOAD: 2000kg
BRAKES: Hydraulic, over-ride, disc
WHEELS: 165/13 - Galvanised, spoked

Options

  • Spare wheel and mount
  • Duofit coupling (17/8 or 50mm capable)
  • Custom ramp options
  • Brake options to increase total GVM to 3500kg
  • Coupling guard
