Medium Boats

Medium Boat Trailers - Quickload

Designed for boats 3.5 to 6.5 metres, these Quickload trailers incorporate the wobble roller system and galvanised leaf springs.

Standard Features

  • Welded steel construction
  • Fully hot-dip galvanised
  • Single axle with leaf springs
  • Wobble roller system
  • Keel roller & plastic skid option
  • Submersible LED lights
  • Jockey wheels standard

GT Trailers provides quality trailer solutions for all your hauling needs. From boat trailers to utility trailers, we build trailers that stand the test of time.
