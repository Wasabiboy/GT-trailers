Handyman Trailer

ECONOMICALLY PRICED FOR THE DOMESTIC MARKET

Fully hot-dip galvanised trailers built for years of reliable use. Utilising our new monocoque construction system, they're much stronger but lighter. Available in six model options from 1.8m x 1.2m to 2.4m x 1.2m, with tipping deck options available.

Contractor Trailer

BUILT FOR COMMERCIAL ENVIRONMENTS

Designed and built to withstand day after day of hard, rugged treatment. Stronger but lighter thanks to monocoque construction. Available in eight model options with tandem axle and tipping options. Perfect for commercial contractors.

Digger Trailer

BEST VERSATILITY AND VALUE-FOR-MONEY

Standard payload of 2050kg, catering for 1.8 tonne machines with extra tools and parts. Fully hot dip galvanised monocoque chassis with hydraulic override disc brakes. All components locally sourced for maximum reliability.
