Jetski Trailers

Jetski Trailers

These high-quality GT Jetski Trailers come in a standard configuration, or an ‘optioned up’ Deluxe model to suit your jetski and lifestyle. Adjustable to suit any jetski, these trailers are constructed in welded steel to last a lifetime. Every trailer is built using New Zealand–sourced components; we can custom-build a solution specifically to suit your machine and lifestyle.

Model options

JSSTD (Standard): Equipped with durable skids for stable support.
JSDEL (Deluxe): Features a full multi-roller system for effortless launching.

Technical specifications (both models)

  • Durability: Welded steel construction, fully hot-dip galvanised.
  • Suspension & axle: Single axle with heavy-duty 1000kg leaf spring suspension.
  • Marine protection: Marine seals on hubs for longevity in salt water.
  • Premium fittings: 3:1 ratio winch, cast lever coupling, and high-tensile safety chain/shackle.
  • Tyres: Wide 18.5 × 8.5 tyres.
  • Visibility: Integrated LED lights on guide poles for safer reversing.
  • Standard inclusion: Quality jockey wheels included.
