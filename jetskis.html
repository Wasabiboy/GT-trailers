Jetski Trailers

Jetski Trailers Built for New Zealand Conditions

GT Trailers manufactures purpose-built jetski trailers in Onehunga, Auckland – engineered for reliable launching and retrieval at boat ramps across New Zealand. Choose from our Standard skid configuration or the Deluxe multi-roller model for effortless loading.


Every trailer is fully adjustable to fit any jetski brand and size. Constructed from welded steel and fully hot-dip galvanised, our jetski trailers are built to withstand years of saltwater exposure, coastal conditions, and regular towing.


All components are sourced within New Zealand, and we offer custom-build options to match your specific machine and towing setup. Whether you’re heading to Tairua, Taupo, or the Hauraki Gulf, a GT jetski trailer gets you on the water safely.

Model options

JSSTD (Standard): Equipped with durable skids for stable support.
JSDEL (Deluxe): Features a full multi-roller system for effortless launching.

Technical specifications (both models)

  • New Zealand fabricated and assembled
  • Welded steel/tread plate construction
  • LED lights 9–33v compatible
  • Fully hot-dip galvanised
  • Leaf spring suspension with tandem axle optional
  • Hydraulic override disc braking system optional
  • Jockey wheel
