Boat Trailers

GT Boat Trailers

Designed and built in New Zealand for New Zealand conditions. Our boat trailers handle saltwater environments and rough roads, from compact single-axle models for 4.5 metre boats through to large tandem-axle trailers for vessels up to 6.9 metres.

All GT boat trailers are fully hot-dip galvanised, fitted with submersible LED lights, quality jockey wheels, and our high-quality wobble roller system for smooth launching and retrieval at any boat ramp.

BOAT TRAILERS

Specialised Boat Trailers For All Vessels

Our boat trailers are designed specifically for New Zealand conditions, built to handle saltwater environments and rough roads. We offer trailers for small dinghies to large vessels, with custom options available. All our boat trailers feature galvanised frames for maximum corrosion resistance.

GT Quickload

Designed for boats 4.5 to 6.9 metres, these GT Quickload trailers from GT Trailers incorporate a high-quality wobble roller system and galvanised leaf springs.

Whether you need a nimble single-axle setup or a robust tandem-axle trailer with advanced braking, our range provides the perfect balance of strength and support for your boat.

Key features

  • Welded steel construction: Fully hot-dip galvanised for maximum durability.
  • Suspension: Single axle with galvanised leaf springs (tandem options available for larger models).
  • Wobble roller system: Standard setup for smooth, easy launching and retrieving.
  • Support options: Keel roller & plastic skid options available to suit your hull.
  • Marine grade: Submersible LED lights.
  • Standard inclusion: Quality jockey wheels included as standard.
