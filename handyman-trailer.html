Handyman Trailer

GT Handyman Trailers

The GT Handyman trailers are fully hot-dip galvanised, built for years of reliable use and economically priced for the domestic market. These trailers utilise our new monocoque construction system. They're much stronger but lighter, using the same system specifications as our heavy-duty Digger and Transport trailers. The wheels are 165/13 light truck tyres on galvanised spoked rims, and the deck is made from 12mm hardwood marine ply. Tie hooks are conveniently located and both tailgates fold down and are easily removed. All componentry is sourced in New Zealand to ensure maximum reliability and availability of parts.

Standard Features

  • Welded steel construction
  • Single axle with leaf springs
  • 12mm hardwood marine ply decks
  • Hinged tailgates & tie hooks around chassis
  • Recessed lights, wiring and plugs to NZ Standards
