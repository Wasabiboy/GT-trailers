Contractor Trailer

GT Contractor Trailers

The GT Contractor Trailers are designed and built to withstand day after day of hard, rugged treatment in the commercial environment. Thanks to the new monocoque construction system, these Contractor Trailers are stronger but lighter. They utilise the same specifications as our heavier duty Digger and Transport Trailers.

Standard Features

  • Welded Treadplate construction
  • Fully galvanised steel
  • Heavy-duty leaf springs
  • 18mm hardwood marine ply decks
  • Jockey Wheel for easy handling & manoeuvring
  • Hinged tailgates & tie hooks around chassis
  • Tipping option - single axle models
  • Tail and front gates become ramps
  • Recessed lights, wiring and plugs to NZ Standards

Eight Model Options

CT8x4 Deck 2.4m x 1.2m
CT8x4TD Tipping Deck 2.4m x 1.2m
CT8x4TA Tandem Axle Deck 2.4m x 1.2m
CT8x5 Deck 2.4m x 1.5m
CT8x5TD Tipping Deck 2.4m x 1.5m
CT8x5TA Tandem Axle Deck 2.4m x 1.5m
CT10x5TA Tandem Axle Deck 3.0m x 1.5m

Optional Extras

  • Tipping Models (single axle only)
  • Galvanised Mesh Cage 1m or 400mm high
  • H-Racks
  • Spare Wheel Mount
