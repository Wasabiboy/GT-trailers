Contractor Trailer

GT Contractor Trailers

GT Contractor Trailers are designed and built to withstand day after day of hard, rugged treatment in the commercial environment. They utilise the same specifications as our heavier duty Digger and Transport Trailers.

Standard Features

  • Welded steel/tread plate construction
  • Fully hot-dip galvanised
  • Single or tandem axle
  • Laminated marine plywood deck
  • Hinged tailgates & tie hooks around chassis

Optional Extras

  • Galvanised mesh cage
  • Spare wheel mount
  • H-racks for long loads
