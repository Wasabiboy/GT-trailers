Contractor Trailers

GT Contractor Trailers: Built for the Professional Tradie

When your livelihood depends on your gear, you need a trailer that works as hard as you do. GT Contractor Trailers are engineered to withstand the daily rigours of the New Zealand commercial environment. Built with the same heavy-duty specifications as our industrial digger and transport ranges, these trailers feature a fully hot-dip galvanised monocoque chassis and a durable marine-grade plywood deck. From the compact CT8x4 workhorse to our high-capacity 2000kg GVM tandem axle models, the GT range offers the ultimate stability and payload for tools, timber, and site materials.

  • Rugged NZ Construction: Fully hot-dip galvanised welded steel and tread plate for maximum durability.
  • Commercial Capacity: Available in single or tandem axle configurations with up to 1600kg load ratings.
  • Versatile Design: Features hinged tailgates and perimeter tie hooks for secure, easy loading.
  • Customisable Setup: Optimise your trailer with optional galvanised mesh cages, H-racks for long loads, or hydraulic braking upgrades.
