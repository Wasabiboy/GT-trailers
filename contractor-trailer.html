Contractor Trailer

GT Contractor Trailers

GT Contractor Trailers are designed and built to withstand day after day of hard, rugged treatment in the commercial environment. They utilise the same specifications as our heavier duty Digger and Transport Trailers.

Standard Features

  • Welded steel/tread plate construction
  • Fully hot-dip galvanised
  • Single or tandem axle
  • Laminated marine plywood deck
  • Hinged tailgates & tie hooks around chassis

Optional Extras

  • Galvanised mesh cage
  • Spare wheel mount
  • H-racks for long loads

CT8x4

The CT8x4 is our entry-level contractor trailer — compact yet tough. Its 2.44m × 1.22m marine ply deck handles everything from garden waste to tools and supplies, while the fully hot-dip galvanised frame keeps it rust-free for years. Perfect for tradies and small contractors needing a reliable daily workhorse.

Product CodeCT8x4
Deck Area2.44m × 1.22m
Load Capacity1,200 kg
AxleSingle
GVM*1,500 kg
Tyre Size185/R14

CT8x5

The CT8x5 gives you extra width over the CT8x4 — the same 2.44m length but a 1.52m deck width — making it ideal when you need to carry wider loads or more gear side by side. Built with the same welded steel and tread plate construction and fully galvanised for long-lasting performance on site.

Product CodeCT8x5
Deck Area2.44m × 1.52m
Load Capacity1,200 kg
AxleSingle
GVM*1,500 kg
Tyre Size185/R14

CT8x5TA

The CT8x5TA is the tandem-axle version of our popular CT8x5, adding a 400kg boost in load capacity and extra stability at speed. With a GVM of 2,000kg it handles heavier commercial payloads with confidence. Hydraulic brake units are available as an optional extra for added control.

Product CodeCT8x5TA
Deck Area2.44m × 1.52m
Load Capacity1,600 kg
AxleTandem*
GVM*2,000 kg
Tyre Size165/R13

CT10x5

Need more length? The CT10x5 stretches to 3.05m while keeping the practical 1.52m width, giving you significantly more deck space for longer materials like timber, pipe, and scaffolding. The single-axle setup keeps towing straightforward while the galvanised chassis ensures long-term durability on the job.

Product CodeCT10x5
Deck Area3.05m × 1.52m
Load Capacity1,200 kg
AxleSingle
GVM*1,500 kg
Tyre Size185/R14

CT10x5TA

The CT10x5TA is our largest and most capable contractor trailer — combining a full 3.05m × 1.52m deck with tandem-axle stability and a 1,600kg load rating. At 2,000kg GVM it's built for serious commercial work. Hydraulic brakes are optionally available, making it safe and capable even on steep terrain.

Product CodeCT10x5TA
Deck Area3.05m × 1.52m
Load Capacity1,600 kg
AxleTandem*
GVM*2,000 kg
Tyre Size165/R13

GVM = Gross Vehicle Mass (total max. weight of trailer & load).  TA = Tandem Axle.
