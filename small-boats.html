Small Boats

Small Boat Trailers

Suitable for a variety of boats from 3.6 to 4.4 metres including RIB and fully inflatable. Versatile T-bar options to full multi-roller.

Standard Features

  • Welded steel construction
  • Fully hot-dip galvanised
  • Single axle with leaf springs
  • Adjustable winch post & handle
  • Adjustable suspension

Optional Extras

Small boat trailers are adjustable with a number of options available:

  • Centre Rollers
  • Wobble Rollers
  • 1:1 or 3:1 Winch

Models

BT360: Beach Trolley for boat up to 3.6m
BT390: For 3.6m to 3.9m boats
BT400: 4m to 4.4m boats

Beach Trolley

Easily launch your Laser, Opti or Inflatable off many types of beach surfaces. Designed for boats up to 3.6 metres.

Fitted with a coupling for low speed towing if necessary. An optional adjustable winch post with snub and handle is available. Load trolley, with boat, onto a standard road trailer.
